Features

Everything you need
for tax season

WiseTransact is a quiet, professional tool — not a flashy fintech app. Built for India, by default.

Manual + SMS entry

Log transactions by hand or paste any bank SMS to auto-fill the amount, mode, and category in one tap.

100% on-device

All your data lives in SQLite on your phone. Nothing is ever sent to a server. No login. No subscription.

Year-end PDF report

Generate a clean, CA-ready PDF of the full Financial Year — categorized, totaled, and ready to share.

Monthly trends

See income vs expenses across months at a glance. Category breakdowns for Food, Transport, Entertainment, and more.

Duplicate detection

Automatically flags potential duplicates when a similar transaction already exists — merge, discard, or keep both.

Multi-account tracking

Track savings, credit cards, wallets, and cash all in one place. Tag transactions to the right account.
